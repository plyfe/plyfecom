Senior Software Engineer

Plyfe, a fast growing, start-up in the digital media space is looking for a Sr. Software Engineer to join our team in NYC to help build our core technologies. Plyfe’s works with Fortune 500 brands helping reach their audiences across web, social and mobile. You will be joining the company as a core team member and a lead developer with great growth opportunities.

We are building a platform to facilitate the creation and deployment of interactive media ad units. Our widgets are embedded across a brand’s digital properties (corporate sites, Facebook, Twitter, etc.). The widgets support interactive content including mini-games, social media actions, polls, and more. User interaction data powers analytics and reporting.

Responsibilities

  • Hands on technology role; contribute to all phases of the software development life cycle, including the technical analysis, design, implementation, and QA
  • Collaborate on key architectural decisions and product considerations
  • Work closely with Co-Founder/CTO
  • Full time position based in our office, located on the lower east side.

Skills

  • A highly skilled individual who has a proven track record and takes pride in bringing high-quality code quickly to market, enjoys learning and applying new technologies, and is passionate about the social media space.
  • Experience developing highly-scalable systems.
  • Entrepreneurial spirit and sense of ownership.
  • Self-motivated to deliver best solutions on schedule.
  • Good communication skills with ability to work closely with peers.
  • Great collaborator who can help define our technology vision.
  • Passionate about great user experience.
  • BS in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or other equivalent
  • At least 5 years industry experience
  • Fluency with Ruby, JavaScript, HTML5/CSS.
  • Experience developing and using external APIs (Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr etc.)
  • Experience working with Cloud technologies (AWS/Rackspace, Heroku etc.)
  • Experience with Algorithms and Data analysis
  • Experience running and scaling at least one of the following databases - MySql, PostgreSQL, MongoDB

Compensation & Perks

Salary commensurate with experience + benefits (medical, dental, vision) + equity. We are offering a stress-free, friendly work environment with great work/life balance. Perks include open vacation policy, flexibility to work from home, equipment budget, office food & beverages, and more.


Send in your cover letter and resume to jobs@plyfe.me.
