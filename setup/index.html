Get your widgets up and running in minutes. . .

  • Markets may have one or both widgets live each week

  • Plyfe will update all Athlete of the Week widgets once per week on Monday mornings. Forms submitted before 9 am ET Monday will have new content live by noon ET Monday. Play of the Week updates will be on a case by case basis for the time being.

  • Visitors may vote once (once per week, or once until widget is updated)

Athlete of the Week

  1. Complete the form below before 9 am ET on Mondays. To reset vote count, Plyfe will set up a new Video Poll challenge for each week.
  2. Upload header image in 300x250 JPG format (visible area is the top 300x94, roll over the example to the right)
  3. Upload choice images in landscape-oriented, 300x154 JPG format. (Don't know if it's the right size? Don't worry, click here to make sure it is!)

Play of the Week

  1. To reset vote count, Plyfe will set up a new Video Poll challenge each time Gannett updates video content.
  2. Since videos will update automatically when markets update their Brightcove players, Plyfe recommend that markets switch off their widgets in Wordpress before updating the video players. After notification from Plyfe that your widget is live, you may reactivate your widget.
  3. For example, when uploading a new video to your Brightcove player:
      Turn widget off. Update Brightcove player. Complete the form below. Switch widget back on after notification from Plyfe that updated widgets are live.
