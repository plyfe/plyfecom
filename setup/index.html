Get your widgets up and running in minutes. . .

  • Each market may have one type of widget live per week.

  • Plyfe will update all widgets once per week on Monday mornings. Forms submitted before 9 am ET Monday will have new content live by noon ET Monday.

  • Visitors may vote once (once per week, or once until widget is updated)

Athlete of the Week

  1. Plyfe will update poll with new content between 9 am and noon ET on Monday each week
  2. Please upload images in 360x300 JPG format

Play of the Week

  1. To reset vote count, Plyfe will set up a new Video Poll challenge for each week
  2. Since videos will update automatically when markets update their Brightcove players, Plyfe recommend that markets switch off their widgets in Wordpress before updating the players. After noon ET on Monday (or after notification from Plyfe that widgets are live), Gannett markets may reactivate widgets on their sites.
  3. For example, if uploading new video to Brightcove on a Saturday:
    a. Turn widget off
    b. Update Brightcove player
    c. Switch widget back on after notification from Plyfe that updated widgets are live
