Plyfe Freemium - What and Why?
Anyone with a digital audience can now utilize the Plyfe Platform by registering at plyfe.me. This free version includes all the cards available in the premium version, the difference being that all published free cards include Plyfe branding as a marketing vehicle to attract new users to our premium offering and Plyfe will own the data. The first premium offering will be to remove the Plyfe branding for $5-25 per year (multiple models to be tested).
To date, our sales approach has been targeted at tier one ad agencies, publishers, and brands. The product market fit has been validated and revenue continues to grow on a monthly basis (currently about 1/3 or burn) but this approach requires longer sales cycles. The goal of the freemium and lower priced tiers is to create an automated sales machine that allows anyone to sign up and swipe their credit card to get started with Plyfe. Goal cost per acquisition lower than ARPU (average revenue per user).
