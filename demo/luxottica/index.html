HOW MUCH OF A TECH LOVER ARE YOU?

  1. You got: wayfarer

    You're edgy, unique, and out there. You've got a lot of talent but not much drive... you are what you are: eccentric. Everyone loves your outlandish characteristics, and some will even adopt them. (Although, being you, you'll probably hate them for it.) Those who know you won't be the least bit surprised when you become the next big thing.

  2. You got: aviator

    You're classic, cool, and laid-back. People envy your natural ability to stay true to yourself. Sometimes others get the impression that you're giving them the cold shoulder... so don't be afraid to open up a little bit. You may find yourself easily annoyed, but just remember that you are surrounded by people who are dying to get to know you.

  3. You got: Sport

    You are athletic, intelligent, and strong-willed. You have what it takes to get the job done... and you will. Life is what you make it and you are determined to make the most of it. Some may be intimidated by your strong opinions, but most are more than willing to embrace them. You can be very independent at times and your friends appreciate that about you.

  4. You got: Granny

    Also known as the John Lennon or Harry Potter glasses, you're a bit of a dork. You find yourself easily obsessed with things... whether it's a new TV show, clothing brand, singer, time period, whatever. Friends adore your oddities, although those who don't know you may be put off by it. You don't like change, but sometimes you refuse to admit it.
