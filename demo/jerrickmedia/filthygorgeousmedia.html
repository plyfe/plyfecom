Filthy Gorgeous’ Things To Do This Weekend: New York City

By Scarlett Belle in advice, Entertainment October 23, 2014

Filthy Gorgeous recommends things to do this weekend in New York City for your pleasure.

As a writer for Filthy Gorgeous, I am always looking for the next big thing that is not only entertaining but sexy. This weekend, I have set my sights on New York City. October 24-26, the city is hosting some of the sexiest events for adult entertainment. This city never sleeps, and for good reason, too. Sex is on high demand and with close to 50 million tourists annually, the city has a big supply of sexy locations to fill any desire.  With New York’s unique combination of power, creativity, and celebrities, the city has a distinct energy and appeal. Whether you’re hipster or high society, this city is one of the ultimate sexual destinations.

1. Exposed: A History of Lingerie

When:

  • October 22 through November 15
  • Tuesday through Friday: Open from Noon-8pm
  • Saturday: Open from 10am-5pm

Where:

The Museum at FIT Seventh Ave Chelsea New York, New York

About:

“Whatever your interest in ladies’ undergarments, you’ll see more than 70 iterations from throughout history at the Museum at FIT’s new exhibit. From a circa-1770 whalebone corset to Rudi Gernreich’s wireless “no-bra bra” of the ’60s, the exhibit looks at how form and function have shifted over time, as well as how nightgowns and other silhouette-framing pieces (like the Juel Park couture favored by Marilyn Monroe and Elizabeth Taylor) have inspired formal wear and everyday clothes alike.” Find out more by visiting their website.
