    Brokerage - Dayton Region

    A Spectrum of Service

    Miller-Valentine Group Realty Services serves your interests by providing a comprehensive, one-stop source of brokerage capabilities. Over the years we have put our experience and expertise to work for clients ranging from Fortune 500 companies to single-tenant industrial users and large multiple site retailers.
    Recent Transactions →

    Market Knowledge

    Our agents bring creative insight and unrivaled depth of market knowledge when helping solve your real estate problems. Additionally, we provide our clients with market-tested, comprehensive real estate data compiled by our market research team. Over the past fifty years, we’ve assisted our customers in buying, selling, or leasing nearly 20 million square feet of retail, industrial, and office space in the greater Dayton area.

    Brokerage Solutions

    We bring an entrepreneurial approach to real estate brokerage. Our real estate sales professionals know how to best represent your needs—whether you’re a real estate investor, a user in a sale, or a tenant in a lease.
    Brokerage Team →

    Standing the Test of Time

    Miller-Valentine Group Realty Services has stood the test of time. Since 1963, our sales professionals have guided our clients through the increasingly complicated maze of zoning regulations, regional planning issues, construction complexities, land sales and lease agreements. This experience allows us to offer insightful industry knowledge and a keen understanding of the local marketplace. We measure our success by your long-term satisfaction.

    Miller-Valentine Group Realty Services offers brokerage services throughout the Dayton, Ohio region.

     

    The Society of Industrial and Office REALTORS® (SIOR) is the leading professional commercial and industrial real estate association. The Society represents today’s most knowledgeable, experienced, and successful commercial real estate brokerage specialists. www.sior.com

     

    A Certified Commercial Investment Member (CCIM) is a recognized expert in the disciplines of commercial investment real estate and is an invaluable resource to the commercial real estate owner, investor and user. www.ccim.com

     

    The National Association of Industrial and Office Properties (NAIOP) is the nation’s leading trade association for developers, owners, investors, asset managers and other professionals in industrial, office and mixed-use commercial real estate. Founded in 1967, NAIOP comprises 14,500+ members in 53 North American chapters. It provides networking opportunities, educational programs, research on trends and innovations and strong legislative representation. www.naiop.org
