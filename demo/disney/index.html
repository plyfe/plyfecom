Quiz: Which Pixar Duo Are You and Your BFF?

Posted 2 days ago
FacebookTwitterGoogle+TumblrPinterest

In the wise words of Marlin, “With fronds like these, who needs anemones?” Take this quiz and share it with your BFF to find out where you two belong in the Pixar world.




























HOW MUCH OF A TECH LOVER ARE YOU?

  1. You got: You’re Carl & Russell!

    As you and your BFF know, you never know where a friendship can begin. But just like Carl and Russell, you and your BFF are driven by the spirit of adventure. Whether exploring the neighborhood or traveling to far and distant places, you can go anywhere with your BFF by your side.

  2. You got: You’re WALL·E & EVE!

    Just like WALL·E and EVE, you and your BFF are always up for trying new things and seeing new places. There’s never a dull moment when you guys are together. There’s lots of world out there, and you two are destined to explore it.

  3. You got: You’re Dory & Marlin!

    You two are just the right amount of silly and serious. Best of all, when you’re feeling down, your BFF knows exactly what to say to cheer you up. With your BFF by your side, you know you can just keep swimming through any obstacle.

  4. You got: You’re Woody & Buzz!

    You and your BFF may not have started out on the best of terms, but you quickly got over your differences. Now, you’re loyal friends who would never leave each other behind. You’ll be there for each other through it all. When you say, “best friends forever,” you definitely mean “forever.”
