Actionable Data

3 API Options on top of the Plyfe reports dashboard

1. Build 1st Party Audience Profiles

Add depth to existing audience profiles to inform your business, content and communication strategies and unlock new retargetting potential. Integrate data from Plyfe card interactions with your existing database and audience profiles via Plyfe Webhooks API.

2. Integrate with 3rd Party Tracking

See all of your data collated in one place, making analysis and optimization that much easier. Add pixels to Plyfe card interactions to track each and every move your audience makes and preference they stake, integrating this data with your existing 3rd party tracking tools.

3. Real-Time Data Benefits

Dynamically respond to audience interactions in real-time to provide contextual experiences. Utilize the Plyfe JavaScript API to monitor interactions with and data from your Plyfe cards in real-time. For example, use Plyfe cards as advertisements or sponsored units as an option for your audience to interact with to unlock content or an experience, opening new opportunities for monetizing your content.
