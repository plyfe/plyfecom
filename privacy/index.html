We use third party service providers to assist us in managing and administering communications and emails sent to us through the Website and through some Plyfe managed sites. Any personal information disclosed through such communications will be governed by such third party vendors’ privacy policy. 

Plyfe provides a number of tools that will allow our customers to promote their website (or articles on their website) over email. Email addresses entered into these may be stored in our database, and  may be stored in our system logs in text format to assist us in tracking down abusive users (users flooding the system), or using these tools for spamming, or any other use we deem nefarious. We will never contact addresses logged in this manner.

Hosted websites

Users hosting websites with Plyfe are given tools that may ask you to optionally enter your email address when making comments on their website, or contacting them through their website. In addition, once in our system, website owners will have the opportunity to review and modify any information entered into their website. Since we do not have full control over how members run their individual sites, this privacy policy does not apply to any hosted Plyfe website that you do not own. You should check that you trust the owner of a particular website before submitting personally identifiable information on that site.

When accessing web pages on the Website, Plyfe may log your IP address, access URL, time of access, geographic location, browser type, and referral URL in order to provide us with internal information regarding site traffic statistics. This information may also be used to identify users who are using the service for spam or other activity that is deemed in conflict with our Terms of Service. Otherwise, this information is used in the aggregate, and is never linked back to any personally identifiable information.
